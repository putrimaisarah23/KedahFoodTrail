Overstepped Cafe

Overstepped Cafe is a casual café in Sungai Petani that offers a relaxed hangout environment with a mix of local and Western-style food and drinks.

The menu usually includes light meals, snacks, coffee, and refreshing beverages, making it suitable for quick meals, study sessions, or casual meetups with friends.

It is known as a comfortable neighbourhood café where customers can chill and enjoy simple, affordable café-style dining in a laid-back atmosphere.

Restaurant Information

📍 Location: No. 5, Tingkat 1, Jalan Perdana Heights 1/1, Perdana Heights, 08000 Sungai Petani, Kedah.

🕒 Opening Hours: 12:00 PM - 11:45 PM (Tue-Sun)

💰 Price Range: RM20 – RM40 (per person)

⭐ Rating: 4.4/ 5
