Merbok Roasted

Merbok Roasted is a local restaurant in Sungai Petani known for serving roasted-style dishes in a casual and comfortable dining environment.

The restaurant is especially recognized for its roasted chicken, rice meals, and other local favorites, offering satisfying portions suitable for lunch and dinner with family and friends.

With its simple atmosphere and focus on everyday comfort food, it has become a convenient choice for diners looking for tasty local meals at affordable prices.

Restaurant Information

📍 Location: Directly opposite UiTM Kedah campus entrance, Merbok.

🕒 Opening Hours: 4:30 AM - 2:00 AM (Closed Fridays)

💰 Price Range: RM1 – RM20 (per person)

⭐ Rating: 4.0/ 5
