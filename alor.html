Alor Setar Breakfast Station

Alor Setar Breakfast Station is a casual breakfast spot in Alor Setar that serves a variety of local Malaysian morning favourites in a relaxed dining environment.

It is known for simple, comforting meals such as nasi lemak, toast, noodles, and traditional kopi-style drinks, making it a popular place for daily breakfast and light meals.

The restaurant focuses on affordable, everyday food with a homely feel, attracting locals and families who prefer a quick and satisfying start to the day.

Restaurant Information

📍 Location: 24, 3, K8, Taman Puspa Kencana, 05460 Alor Setar, Kedah, Malaysia.

🕒 Opening Hours: 7:30 AM - 12:30 PM (Daily)

💰 Price Range: RM1 – RM20 (per person)

⭐ Rating: 4.3/ 5



🍽 Signature Menu

Nasi Lemak Ayam

Nasi lemak ayam is a rice cooked in coconut milk served with fried chicken, sambal, egg, and anchovies.

RM14.70

Telur Kapit

Telur kapit is a crispy folded egg snack, often served as a light bite or side dish.

RM7.90

Roti Goyang

Roti goyang is a toast with soft-boiled “runny” egg, usually eaten with soy sauce and pepper.

RM10.50

⭐ My Review

Alor Setar Breakfast Station is an excellent place for breakfast lovers. The restaurant offers a unique combination of local and Western-inspired breakfast dishes at affordable prices. The Set Nasi Lemak Ayam is flavorful and filling, while the Roti Teloq Kapit and Roti Goyang provide a simple yet enjoyable morning meal. The service is friendly, the atmosphere is comfortable, and the food quality is consistently good. Overall, it is highly recommended for anyone looking for a satisfying breakfast experience in Alor Setar.
