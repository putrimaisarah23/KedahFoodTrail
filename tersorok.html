Tersorok Cafe

Tersorok Cafe is a hidden-style café located in Sungai Petani that offers a calm and relaxed dining experience away from the busy crowd. It is known for its cozy, “hidden gem” concept that attracts customers looking for a quiet place to eat and chill.

The café serves a mix of local Malaysian dishes, light Western meals, snacks, and drinks, making it suitable for breakfast, lunch, or casual hangouts. Its menu is simple but satisfying, focusing on comfort food and easy dining options.

Tersorok Cafe is also popular among locals for its peaceful atmosphere and affordable meals, making it a comfortable spot for friends, families, and solo visitors who want a relaxed food experience.

Restaurant Information

📍 Location:Bandar Puteri Jaya, 08000 Sungai Petani, Kedah.

🕒 Opening Hours: 8:30 AM - 11:30 AM (Mon-Thu) & 11:30 AM - 2:30 PM (Sat-Sun)

💰 Price Range: RM1 – RM20 (per person)

⭐ Rating: 4.6 / 5



🍽 Signature Menu

Mee Kari & Laksa Kedah

Mee Kari is a creamy coconut curry noodle dish served with noodles, chicken or seafood, and simple toppings like egg and tofu.
Laksa Kedah is a traditional Kedah noodle dish with a rich, sour fish-based broth served with rice noodles and fresh herbs.

RM10.80 & RM9.80

Matcha Cake Batik & Biscoff Burnt Cheese Cake

Matcha Cake Batik is a layered dessert combining traditional cake batik with a mild matcha flavor for a sweet and slightly earthy taste.
Biscoff Burnt Cheesecake is a creamy burnt cheesecake topped or mixed with Biscoff spread, giving a rich caramelized and slightly spiced flavor.

RM9.80 & RM13.80

Nasi Lemak & Nasi Kerabu

Nasi Lemak is a classic Malaysian dish of coconut milk rice served with sambal, fried anchovies, peanuts, boiled egg, and cucumber.
Nasi Kerabu is a traditional Kelantan-style rice dish usually served with herbs, salted fish or fried chicken, and a variety of spicy sambal.

RM5.80 & RM12.80
