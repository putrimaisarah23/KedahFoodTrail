Sheikh Bistro

Sheikh Bistro is a well-known casual dining restaurant in Sungai Petani that serves a variety of Malaysian daily meals in a comfortable and family-friendly environment.

Known for its wide range of local favorites, the restaurant offers rice dishes, noodles, roti-based meals, and other popular Malaysian comfort foods, making it a convenient choice for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

With its relaxed atmosphere and diverse menu selection, Sheikh Bistro has become a popular spot for families, friends, and customers looking for everyday local dining options.

Restaurant Information

📍 Location: 7, Jalan Jati 1, Kompleks Perniagaan Amanjaya, 08000 Sungai Petani, Kedah, Malaysia.

🕒 Opening Hours: 7:00 AM - 1:00 AM (Daily)

💰 Price Range: RM1 – RM20 (per person)

⭐ Rating: 4.1/ 5
