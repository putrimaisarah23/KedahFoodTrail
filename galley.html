Big Galley's Cafe

Big Galley’s Cafe is a popular café-style restaurant in Alor Setar that serves a mix of Western, Asian, and local fusion dishes in a casual dining environment.

Known for its generous portions and wide menu variety, the restaurant offers grilled meals, pasta, rice dishes, snacks, and desserts, making it a favourite spot for families and group gatherings.

Big Galley’s Cafe is also well known for its comfortable seating and relaxed atmosphere, making it a suitable place for both casual dining and social meetups.

Restaurant Information

📍 Location: 7 Ground Floor, Kompleks Perniagaan LITC, 05150 Alor Setar, Kedah, Malaysia.

🕒 Opening Hours: 10:00 AM – 11:30 PM (Tuesday-Sunday)

💰 Price Range: RM20 – RM40 (per person)

⭐ Rating: 4.1 / 5
