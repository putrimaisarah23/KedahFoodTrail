Morning Kedah Restaurant

Morning Kedah Restaurant is a local restaurant in Alor Setar that serves a variety of traditional Malaysian dishes in a casual and family-friendly dining environment.

Known for its breakfast and daily meal selections, the restaurant offers local favorites such as porridge, noodle dishes, rice meals, and other comforting Malaysian foods that suit different tastes.

With its simple atmosphere and affordable menu, it has become a convenient spot for customers looking for everyday meals and a relaxed dining experience.

Restaurant Information

📍 Location: 8B, Tingkat Bawah Jalan Tengah, Off Jalan Tunku Abdul Rahman Putra, 05100 Alor Setar, Kedah, Malaysia.

🕒 Opening Hours: 7:30 AM - 10:00 PM (Daily)

💰 Price Range: RM20 – RM40 (per person)

⭐ Rating: 4.5/ 5

⭐ My Review

Morning Kedah Restaurant offers a pleasant dining experience with a wide selection of local dishes. The Mee Kari is rich and flavorful, while the Nasi Sambal provides a satisfying balance of spicy and savory tastes. The restaurant is clean, affordable, and suitable for family meals. Overall, it is a recommended place for anyone who wants to experience authentic Kedah cuisine.
